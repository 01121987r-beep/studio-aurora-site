Cookie Policy

Information on the use of cookies and similar technologies while browsing this website.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on the user's device while visiting a website. They can improve functionality, performance, and analytics.

Types of cookies used

Technical cookies: necessary for website operation.
Analytics cookies: collect aggregated and anonymized traffic data.
Third-party cookies: for example embedded maps and external services.

Legal basis and consent

Technical cookies are used based on legitimate interest. For non-essential cookies, consent is collected through a dedicated banner when implemented.

Managing preferences

Users can modify or withdraw consent at any time through browser settings or through the cookie management system adopted by the website.

Third-party services

The website may embed external services (e.g., Google Maps). Such services may set their own cookies, governed by each provider's policy.
