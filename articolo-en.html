Chronic stress: signs, daily impact, and practical strategies

Understanding early warning signs of stress helps you intervene early and reduce the risk of psycho-physical exhaustion.

When stress becomes persistent

Stress is a useful physiological response in challenging situations. It becomes problematic when it remains active for weeks or months, creating a constant state of alert.

Common signs

Frequent signs include irritability, sleep issues, concentration problems, physical tension, and reduced motivation. A critical inner dialogue and a persistent feeling of "never enough time" are also common.

Three actions to start today

1. Introduce short but regular breaks during the day.
2. Reduce multitasking and prioritize one task at a time.
3. Build an evening shutdown ritual to help mind and body slow down.

The role of psychological support

Psychological support helps identify the factors maintaining stress, build personalized coping strategies, and restore a sense of personal efficacy.
